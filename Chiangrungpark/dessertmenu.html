Dessert

Desserts are sweet foods consumed after the main meal. The term "dessert" encompasses sweet foods that primarily consist of sugar and carbohydrates (confections), such as biscuits, cakes, cookies, ice cream, and extends to beverages like sweet wines and liqueurs. Desserts vary from country to country.
