Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a type of pasta characterized by its long, thin, cylindrical shape. It's a staple in Italian cuisine and is often served with a variety of sauces, from marinara and meatballs to creamy Alfredo. The pasta is typically boiled until al dente, creating a satisfying texture, and it's loved for its versatility and ability to pair with various flavors and ingredients.
